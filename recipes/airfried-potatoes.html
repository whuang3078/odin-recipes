Air Fried Potatoes

Description

For all the french fry lovers out there who don't want excessive oil calories nor losing too much potato satiety whilst preserving flavoring, this one's for you :)

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (1 lb)
  • Cooking Spray
  • Sea Salt (as needed)
  • Black Pepper (as needed)
  • Garlic Powder (as needed)
  • Italian Seasoning (as needed)
  • Ketchup (or sub whatever condiment)

Directions

  1. Rinse and scrub potatoes in sink
  2. Dice potatoes into ~1 inch cubes
  3. Put potatoes into air fryer
  4. Spray with cooking spray and shake potatoes around until coated
  5. Add desired amounts of salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  6. Shake potatoes until are evenly coated in seasoning
  7. Air fry for 15 min at 400F, stirring potatoes at ~8min to ensure even cooking
  8. Serve with your favorite condiment!
