Milkfoam

Description

Basically just salty sweet heavy whipping cream, this stuff is straight crack. It's basically like buttercream icing.

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk (1 cup)
  • Heavy Whipping Cream (1 quart)
  • Sea Salt (1 tsp)
  • Cream Powder (2 tbsp)
  • Sugar (1 cup)

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients into mixing bowl
  2. Mix for 18 minutes
