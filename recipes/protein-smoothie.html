Protein Smoothie

Description

This served as one of my staple cutting meals for whenever I've gotten ready for photoshoots or just plain shredded.

Incredibly filling, cheap, delicious, and most importantly, easy as boiling pasta to prepare (though much faster to ready), it's quite obvious why I loved this meal.

Ingredients

  • MyProtein Chocolate Casein (1-2 scoops)
  • MyProtein Salted Caramel Whey (1-2 scoops)
  • Rolled Oats (1/4 cup)
  • Banana
  • Peanut Butter (1-2 tbsp)
  • Almond Milk (as needed, ~2 cups)
  • Crushed Ice (1 cup)

Directions

Note: Reverse ingredient ordering if using Nutribullet
  1. Add half amount of almond milk into blender
  2. Add whey and casein into blender
  3. Add oats into blender
  4. Add peanut butter into blender
  5. Peel banana
  6. Add banana into blender
  7. Add ice into blender
  8. Add rest of almond milk into blender
  9. Blend on high until consistent texture, and from there, can add time depending on desired viscosity
